Moongoose

Indian mongoose are popularly used to fight and kill venomous snakes, because of their rapid reactions and thick coat.

  • Scientific Name: Herpestidae
  • Average Length: 55 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 7-12 years
  • Habitat: Open forests and grass plains

Mongoose is the popular English name for 29 of the 34 species in the 14 genera of the family Herpestidae, which are small feliform carnivorans that are native to southern Eurasia and mainland Africa.
